About Nike Kenya

Our Mission

"To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in Kenya. If you have a body, you are an athlete."

The Kenyan Legacy

Kenya is globally recognized as the home of champions. From the highlands of Iten to the bustling streets of Nairobi, running is more than just a sport—it is part of the cultural fabric.

Nike Kenya was established to ensure that local athletes, enthusiasts, and dreamers have direct access to the same elite footwear that crosses the finish line first at major world marathons.

Nike Kenya started with a clear vision to uplift local athletic talent.
It allows every athlete across Kenya to access professional gear with ease.
It operates across all 47 counties, from Nairobi all the way to Turkana.
It promotes fitness, national pride, and a lasting culture of excellence.

Why We Do What We Do

We believe in the power of sport to move the world forward.

We strive to break down barriers to entry for high-performance gear.

We are committed to sustainability and protecting the future of sport.
